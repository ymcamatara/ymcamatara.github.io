About YMCA Matara

YMCA Matara is part of the global YMCA movement, the world's oldest and largest youth organization founded in 1844. Our branch is located at 9C, Station Approach Road, serving the vibrant coastal city of Matara in the Southern Province of Sri Lanka.

For decades, we have been at the forefront of youth development, providing safe spaces, meaningful programs, and opportunities for young people to grow, learn, and lead. Our work spans across sports, education, community service, and leadership development.

We believe in the potential of every young person and work tirelessly to help them discover their strengths, build their skills, and become responsible citizens who contribute positively to society.
